Project Overview

Scarlet Agent represents the next generation of intelligent personal assistants, built specifically for the modern academic and professional ecosystem. Our platform seamlessly integrates with your existing tools and workflows to provide productivity enhancement through advanced AI capabilities.

Born from the innovation labs at Rutgers University, Scarlet Agent combines cutting-edge natural language processing, machine learning, and intuitive user experience design to create an assistant that truly understands and anticipates your needs.

Whether you’re managing class schedules, collaborating with teams, or organizing your digital workspace, Scarlet Agent adapts to your unique workflow patterns and preferences— learning and evolving with you to maximize your academic potential.
